About Vertex Academy

At Vertex Academy, we believe in shaping strong academic foundations from Class 1 to Class 10. Our goal is not just to help students score marks, but to help them understand, grow, and love learning.

Founded by Mr. Deepak Ghatkar, a passionate educator with over 25 years of experience. He completed his graduation in Textile Engineering from the prestigious VJTI (Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute). With a deep love for teaching and mentoring, he has helped thousands of students unlock their potential through personal guidance and consistent support.

With a perfect blend of modern teaching techniques, personalized attention, and regular assessments — we ensure each child gets the care and clarity they deserve.
